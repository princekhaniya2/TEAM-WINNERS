Resources & Facilities

Most Appealing Premises: Uniglobe Secondary School operates in a cost-effective yet highly appealing environment with ultra-modern facilities. From beautiful study parks to indoor ambiance, students experience peaceful study settings throughout the year. The campus is centrally air-conditioned and decorated to feel like home.

Conference Hall

With over 300-seat capacity, the conference hall hosts co-curricular activities, talk shows, presentations, and more. It is equipped with AC, multimedia, and a modern stage setup.

Library

The library is packed with books on science and management, encyclopedias, periodicals, multimedia, and access to e-library platforms to enhance learning.

Computer Lab

Uniglobe's computer lab features branded LED computers with full internet access, cloud-based learning technology, and hands-on experience for science and management students.

Cafeteria

Focused on hygiene and nutrition, the cafeteria serves quality local and continental food. It supports students' mental and physical health by providing balanced meals at reasonable prices.

Sports

UGSS offers both indoor and outdoor sports like futsal, cricket, badminton, basketball, and table tennis. Students enjoy both competition and non-competition-based physical activities for health and motivation.

Transportation

Student-friendly buses and microbuses are available for convenient transportation within the Kathmandu ring road area.

Class Rooms

Modern classrooms are fully air-conditioned, equipped with multimedia, fire alarms, internet, and smart-class designs for effective learning.
