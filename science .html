+2 Science Program

Your Gateway to a Successful Future

Uniglobe Secondary School +2 Science program is a dynamic hub for aspiring leaders. Students embark on a comprehensive journey, equipped with real-world applicable skills through interactive exercises, case studies, and simulations. Modern classrooms support critical thinking, problem-solving, and teamwork in a collaborative learning environment.

Course Composition in +2 Science

Grade XI

  • Compulsory English
  • Compulsory Nepali
  • Compulsory Mathematics
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
  • Optional: Biology / Computer Science

Grade XII

  • Compulsory English
  • Compulsory Nepali
  • Compulsory Mathematics
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
  • Optional: Biology / Computer Science

Eligibility

Minimum SEE GPA of 1.6, with at least D+ in English, Nepali, and Mathematics. Exposure to Social Studies and Science is desirable.

Admission Process

  • Step 1 - Get Counselling: Personalized guidance based on academic background and goals.
  • Step 2 - Fill Admission Form: Available online or at the office.
  • Step 3 - Entrance Exam: Access practice papers and sample questions.
  • Step 4 - Enroll: Submit documents on time for smooth enrollment.
